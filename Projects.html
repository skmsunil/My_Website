Online Exam Portal

As a part of Mini Project, Myself along with 3 other classmate as team we created the Online Exam Portal. The aim for this portal was to conduct the exams online.As there are various test are being conducted on Monthly basis in colleges. To reduce the time, effort which takes to conduct the exam and predominantly paper where its mostly wasted for writing Story rather than main points by most of the pupil in the exam.

The Website was created by using HTML, css, JS, BootStrap, PHP, Mysqli, Jquery. The Assessment was in the form of Multiple choice Question(MCQ). As soon as the student start the test, the time will be started and the Question will be loaded from the XML file. The Question was being displayed randomly for every student. Based on the number of question the time was allocated. And As soon as the time gets over, the user will be logged out, marked answer gets submitted and the result was displayed.
